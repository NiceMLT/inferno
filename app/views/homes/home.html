Past Season Information

2018 Fall League

Fall League is a great opportunity for new players of all skill levels and athletic backgrounds to come out and learn how to play ultimate in a fun and helpful setting. A $35 registration cost covers a Brookwood Ultimate disc and reversible tank jersey for each player. Teams will be formed by the coaches taking into account skill level and gender ratios.

Each week there will be an hour long clinic from 4:30 p.m. - 5:30 p.m. where we'll teach players the rules, throws, and basic strategy. Then each week from 5:30 p.m. - 6:30 p.m. there will be a 1-hour game against another of our teams.

We'll clinic/play every Wednesday at Crews Middle School starting September 5th through November 14th (excluding October 3rd for fall break). That's 10 weeks of clinic and games!
