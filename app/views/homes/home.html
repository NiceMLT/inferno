2019 Fall League!

Welcome potential Ultimate players. Brookwood Ultimate is excited to announce our Fall League will start on 9/4 and run until 11/20. Registration is FREE for new players. This includes a custom Brookwood Ultimate frisbee on your second day!!!


  • Who
    • - Any 8th - 12th grade students @ Brookwood/cluster schools - Exceptions can be made for non-Brookwood or 7th grade participants on a case by case basis.
  • Schedule
    • - Every Wednesday from 9/4/19 - 11/20/10 4:30 - 6:30 p.m.
  • Location
    • - Crews Middle School field - We may utilize Craig Elem. or Five Forks Middle at times
  • Cost
    • - NEW PLAYERS FREE!!! Returning players: $10 - This includes one Brookwood Ultimate disc! - Cash or paypal brookwood.ultimate@gmail.com

You don't have to be able to attend every week to participate. Please fill out the form and/or contact us at brookwood.ultimate@gmail.com with questions.
