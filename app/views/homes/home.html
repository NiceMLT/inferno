Getting better over the summer

There's a lot of opportunities to improve over the summer as well

  • Atlanta Hustle - The local professional men's team hosts summer camps for both new and experienced players: http://www.atlantahustle.com/camps-clinics/
  • Spin Ultimate Academy - A local summer camp to help players both learn and refine their skils: http://spinultimateacademy.com/
  • Weekly local (in Snellville) pick-up ultimate. Returning Brookwood Ultimate players will be hosting pick-up ultimate for current/former players each week. Fill out the form below to get information on when/where that happens

2018-2019 Season

We'll run a league in the fall for Brookwood students on Wednesdays starting in September (with Sign-ups in August). This low-cost league (around $20) will be a great opportunity for players to learn the game as well as have returning players refine their skills. The league includes a reversible jersey and disc and is a great entry point for new players.
