2019 Rising 9th Grade Information

Welcome rising 9th graders! Brookwood Ultimate is excited to partner with Crews Middle and Five Forks Middle this year to offer ultimate frisbee "Learn to Play" clinics at your school(s). Come join us at Five Forks Middle on 4/11 and 4/16 immediately after school and/or Crews Middle on 4/10 and 4/17 immediately after school from 4:30-6:00 to learn how to play ultimate frisbee. We'll also be giving away discs and ultimate frisbee gear (jerseys, shorts, hats) at these clinics!


Fill out the form below so we can remind you about these events and let you know about any other summer or fall ultimate when you get to Brookwood!
